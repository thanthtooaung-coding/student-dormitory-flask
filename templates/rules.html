Community Rules

Please read and follow these community guidelines

General Rules

  • Respect all residents and staff members
  • Keep noise levels reasonable, especially during quiet hours (10 PM - 7 AM)
  • Maintain cleanliness in common areas
  • No smoking or alcohol consumption in dormitory premises
  • Follow all safety and security protocols

Room Regulations

  • Keep your room clean and organized
  • Report any damages or maintenance issues immediately
  • No unauthorized room modifications
  • Respect roommate privacy and space
  • Follow check-in and check-out procedures

Visitor Policy

  • All visitors must be registered in advance
  • Visitors are allowed only during designated hours (8 AM - 10 PM)
  • Residents are responsible for their visitors' behavior
  • Overnight visitors require prior approval

Facility Usage

  • Book facilities in advance through the system
  • Clean up after using common facilities
  • Report any facility issues or damages
  • Follow facility-specific rules and guidelines

Payment & Bills

  • Pay all bills by the due date
  • Late payments may incur penalties
  • Contact management for payment issues
  • Keep receipts for all payments
